Load Makerlabs Post Processor into Fusion Cloud

  1. Open Preferences.



  2. Go to: General/Maufacture and check Enable Cloud Libraries.





  3. Open your Data Panel (Left sidebar) and under LIBRARIES open the Assets folder.



  4. Open CAMPost folder.



  5. Click Upload. Choose the Post Processor file by clicking on Select Files or Drag and Drop your files â€¦finish by clicking Upload



  6. Open the Post Processor click Setup and choose UseCloudPost.
    As the post processor is uploaded to the cloud you can access it from all machines as long as you log in to your Fusion 360 account.
